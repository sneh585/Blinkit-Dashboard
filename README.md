Project Title 
Blinkit-Dashboard


Short Description / Purpose

The Blinkit Retail Analytics Dashboard is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to help users explore and understand grocery sales performance across different outlet types, locations, and product categories. The dashboard focuses on key retail metrics such as total sales, average sales, item distribution, outlet size performance, and customer ratings. This tool is intended for business analysts, retail managers, and data enthusiasts who want to identify sales trends, evaluate outlet performance, and gain actionable insights to support data-driven decision making in the quick commerce retail industry.


Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

 📊 *Power BI Desktop* – Primary data visualization platform used to design the interactive dashboard and create business insights.

 📂 *Power Query* – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation before building the data model.

 🧠 *DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)* – Applied to create calculated measures such as Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and dynamic aggregations for better analysis.

  📝 *Data Modeling* – Structured relationships between dataset fields such as outlet type, outlet size, item category, and sales metrics to enable efficient filtering and analysis.

 📁 *File Formats* – .pbix used for Power BI dashboard development and .png used for dashboard preview images in the GitHub repository.

 
• Business Problem

The quick commerce grocery industry is highly competitive and generates large volumes of sales data across different outlet locations and product categories. However, businesses often struggle to quickly analyze which outlets perform best, which product types drive the most revenue, and how factors such as outlet size, location, and product fat content impact overall sales performance.

Key questions such as:
Which outlet locations generate the highest sales?
Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
Do outlet sizes influence total sales and product demand?
How do customer ratings and product attributes affect performance?


 • Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive business intelligence dashboard that:

* Provides a clear overview of Blinkit’s sales performance.
* Enables users to explore sales trends across outlet types, sizes, and locations.
* Helps identify top-performing product categories and product attributes.
* Supports data-driven decision making for retail strategy and operational improvements.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

*Key KPIs (Top Section)*
Displays important business metrics such as:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items Sold
* Average Customer Rating

These KPIs give a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

*Outlet Establishment Trend (Line Chart)*
Shows how outlet establishments have evolved over time, helping identify expansion trends and business growth patterns.

*Sales by Outlet Size (Donut Chart)*
Visualizes sales distribution across small, medium, and large outlets, highlighting which outlet size contributes the most to revenue.

*Sales by Outlet Location (Bar Chart)*
Compares sales performance across different outlet location tiers, helping identify high-performing regions.

*Item Type Analysis (Bar Chart)*
Displays sales contribution by product categories such as fruits, snacks, dairy, and household items, helping identify top-selling product segments.

*Fat Content Distribution (Donut Chart)*
Compares sales of low-fat vs regular products, helping understand customer preferences in product health attributes.

*Outlet Type Performance (Table / Chart)*
Breaks down total sales, average sales, item counts, and ratings across different outlet types to evaluate operational efficiency.

 • Business Impact & Insights

*Product Strategy:* Businesses can identify top-performing product categories and focus inventory planning accordingly.

*Outlet Performance Analysis:* Helps management understand which outlet sizes and locations generate the highest revenue.

*Customer Preference Insights:* Fat content and rating analysis highlight consumer buying behavior and product preferences.
